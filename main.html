The origins of Lorem Ipsum

About Its Source

From simply reading the average lay scholar could assume that it is Latin.
Where from though is beyond most people's recognition. Well as usual Wikipedia has the answer.
It is from a corrupted translation of a book called De finibus bonorum et malorum.
Loosely translated as "On the ends of good and evil."
This otherwise boring treatise has wormed itself into contemporary society.

About Its Creator

For the most part the ancient, medieval, and rennisance era were populated by people who studied everything
One of these people was Cicero. Born rich (a good start) he proceeded to run a real estate empire
when he was not lecturing in the Roman Senate. He was also considered an elite
philosopher whose writings were preserved by medieval christian
and Islamic scholars. He was eventually killed when he took the wrong side in Julius Caesar's political scheming.
